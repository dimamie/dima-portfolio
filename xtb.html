Fintech projects @ xtb

Financial instrument specification, discovery, and regulatory transparency

Users need to quickly verify whether a specific financial instrument is available before committing to the app, while regulators require full, standardized disclosure.

This public instrument specification presents complete legal data per instrument in a clear, scannable format that works for both pre-signup users and compliance needs.

Previously, users had to dig through multiple accordion-hidden tables to find a single instrument.

All instruments are now unified into one searchable table, making exploration and lookup significantly faster.

Wide data tables are difficult to use on mobile devices.

Mobile version restructures the table into instrument-level accordions, keeping navigation usable on small screens.

Standard industry rollover tables span multiple months and are hard to read at a glance.

A calendar-based monthly view makes upcoming rollovers easy to scan and reason about.

Advanced instrument concepts are often confusing and poorly explained, forcing users to leave the page to understand them.

Contextual tooltips provide clear, on-demand explanations without breaking the browsing flow.
